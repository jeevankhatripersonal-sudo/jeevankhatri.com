About

The person
behind the mix

I'm Jeevan Khatri — a mixing and mastering engineer and music producer with ten years of professional experience. I've built my studio operation from the ground up, working with artists across the globe.

In a decade of doing this, I've had the privilege of working alongside multi-platinum producers, with tracks I've engineered accumulating millions of streams internationally. I hold a certificate from Berklee Online and have continued building my craft every year since.

I'm not just someone who knows the technical side. I understand music. That's what separates a technically clean mix from one that actually moves people.

"I don't just process audio. I make sure every decision serves the song."
